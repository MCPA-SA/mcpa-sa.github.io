GlobalHackIV

On Sunday, June 7th two members of our Chapter won second place as part of a three man team in GlobalhackIV! Over the course of one weekend the team developed an app for adding a "Choose your own adventure" style game to the social networking site lockerdome.com. The competition started at 1800 on Friday, June 5th and ran until the judging began at noon on Sunday. The team received $15,000 for winning 2nd place. See lockerdome.com/globalhack and this press release for more details and pictures.
